Patriotic Visit

Park Safely

Please keep driveways, intersections, and mailboxes clear while enjoying the display.

💡

When parked, dim your headlights so the lights and effects can be enjoyed by everyone.

🇺🇸

Visitor Checklist

Quick Reminders

  • Keep music at a neighbor-friendly volume.
  • Do not block driveways, mailboxes, or intersections.
  • Take photos and videos safely, away from traffic.
  • Watch carefully for families, children, pets, and passing vehicles.
  • Share the red, white, and blue spirit with family and friends.
